About the Tinyman v1.0

Beginning on the 1st of January 2022, an attack was orchestrated by unauthorized users on some of Tinyman’s pools by exploiting a previously unknown vulnerability in the Tinyman contracts. Since then, the contracts have been updated to V1.1. If you were an LP at that time, make sure to migrate your assets.

Please check if you had remaining liquidity and redeemable amounts from Tinyman v1.0 for your safety.Those pools will not be used within Tinyman anymore.
